Doraemon Characters

Nobita Nobi

Nobita is a kind-hearted boy who often struggles with his studies and is bullied by his classmates.

Doraemon

Doraemon is a robotic cat from the future who helps Nobita with his gadgets and advice.

Shizuka Minamoto

Shizuka is Nobita's love interest and is known for her intelligence and kindness.

Suneo Honekawa

Suneo is a wealthy boy who often teases Nobita but is also a loyal friend.

Takeshi Gouda (Gian)

Gian is a big and strong boy who loves to sing, though he's not very good at it!

Doraemon's Friends

Doraemon's friends include Nobita, Shizuka, Suneo, and Gian, forming a close-knit group.
