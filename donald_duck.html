Donald Duck Characters

Donald Duck

Donald Duck is a quick-tempered, yet lovable, sailor who often finds himself in comical misadventures.

Daisy Duck

Daisy is Donald's elegant and sophisticated girlfriend, known for her charm and wit.

Huey, Dewey, and Louie

Donald's mischievous nephews often join him on adventures, causing both trouble and fun.

Scrooge McDuck

Scrooge is Donald's wealthy uncle, famous for his stinginess and his giant money bin.

Launchpad McQuack

Launchpad is a clumsy yet courageous pilot who helps Donald and his friends on their adventures.

Donald's Friends

Donald's circle includes Mickey Mouse, Goofy, and Pluto, making every adventure unforgettable.
