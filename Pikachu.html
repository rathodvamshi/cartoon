Pikachu & Friends

Pikachu

Pikachu is an electric-type Pokémon known for its cute appearance and powerful Thunderbolt attack.

Ash Ketchum

Ash is Pikachu's best friend and trainer, known for his determination to become a Pokémon Master.

Misty

Misty is Ash's close friend and a skilled Water-type Pokémon trainer.

Brock

Brock is a Pokémon Breeder and Rock-type trainer, always ready to help his friends.

Team Rocket

Jessie, James, and Meowth are always scheming to steal Pikachu for their nefarious plans.

Legendary Pokémon

Pikachu's adventures often bring him face-to-face with legendary Pokémon like Lugia and Ho-Oh.
